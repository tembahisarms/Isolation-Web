Stay Informed

Know your risk when going out in your community or interacting with members of other households. The Isolation Web helps you and the members of your community stay informed about COVID-19’s potential impact on your neighborhood and social network by visualizing the data for you so you can make informed decisions about your social interactions.

We’ll only ask for the bare essentials to the visualization of the web - your email (so we can contact you); your zip code (so your connections can search for you); your name (or top-secret codename!); and your house’s codename (so your connections can identify you). And we won’t share this info with anyone else.

Inform Others

By adding your household to the web, you can share information about you and your household. You can share this information with friends, family, and other members of your community to encourage them to stay informed and make knowledge-based decisions to slow the spread of the pandemic such as wearing masks, washing hands frequently, and practicing social distancing.

Help Keep Each Other Safe

During the COVID-19 health crisis, many find themselves torn between the need to be with loved ones, and the need to separate themselves in order to protect their community. In facing this new reality, we must question not just whether to isolate ourselves, but exactly how much. The Isolation Web hopes to empower people with information about that potential impact and to help them make informed decisions that align with their values.
